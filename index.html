PORTFOLIO

Balldown

A fast-paced, geometrically inspired game where the player navigates a three-dimensional landscape at breakneck speeds. An in-game store, powered by scriptable objects, allows for enhancements and power-ups, adding to the strategic depth. Dynamic, moving objects serve as both obstacles and stepping stones, demanding quick reflexes and split-second decision making. The main challenges during development were the accurate physics modeling and maintaining high frame rates, especially with the ever-changing, moving game objects.

Fantasy Survivors

This game is a mixture of a running and shooting game set in a procedurally generated terrain, offering a unique experience every time it's played. Players face increasingly difficult enemies that spawn randomly across the landscape. Atmospheric shaders create a misty, nebulous backdrop adding to the game's ambiance. The procedural terrain and dynamic fog and cloud shaders proved to be challenging in the development phase, ensuring seamless and coherent game experience across various terrains.

10 Seconds

A 2D platformer game where each level lasts only 10 seconds, hence the name. The game consists of five increasingly challenging iterations of the same level, creating a sense of urgency and increasing difficulty. Designing the escalating difficulty curve, so it was both challenging and fair, was a substantial part of this project's development.

Shoot'em'up

A straightforward but engaging 2D game. Players navigate their character through a barrage of incoming threats, shooting them down and avoiding contact. The art of simplicity was the main challenge for this game's development, creating an enjoyable and replayable experience using limited mechanics and design elements.

TopDungeon

In this top-down 2D game, the player controls a knight who can upgrade their weapon, fight against enemies with distinct idle and attacking states, and collect rewards from chests. The AI design for the enemies and the game's balancing, particularly the weapon upgrades and reward distribution, presented unique development challenges.

Clicker

This game represents an early venture into 2D game development and the world of incremental clicker games. A player clicks to gather resources, enhance their clicking power, and unlock various game features. The challenge in developing this game was balancing progression and rewards, ensuring the game was engaging for newcomers and veterans alike.
